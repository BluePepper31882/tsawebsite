Launch Information

Launch Day is the start of your life changing space travel experience. After completing the mandatory training course, prior to space travel, you will be ready embark on your space voyage. All launch sites come standard with their own on-site training facilities, dormitories and cafeterias for trainees, and on-site muesuems displaying our rich history in the world of Space Tourism.


With our multiple launch sites across the United States, you have the choice to embark from a variety of locations. These locations range from the famous Cape Canaveral, Florida, Mojave Desert, California, Houston Texas, Tuscon, Arizona, and Huntsville, Alabama
