About Section

Our Mission

Our mission is towards to making the dream of space travel a reality. With cutting-edge technology and unparalleled expertise, we offer an experience that takes you beyond the limits of Earth and into the wonders of space. Experience the thrill of zero-gravity, witness the stunning beauty of our planet from above, and be a part of the future of space travel with Space-1.

Our Vision

Space-1's vision is to make space accessible to those who seek it, delivering a truly unique and transformative experience through our innovative technology. Our mission is to provide an extraordinary journey to the final frontier and inspire a new generation of space enthusiasts.

About Safety

Space -1 maintains a high level of diligence when it comes to safety. Our technology and methods used to launch our customers into space have been thoroughly tested and refined over the years. We have invested heavily in the development of safe and reliable spacecrafts. 3 billion dollars has been allocated to the safety features of our space shuttles, customer training facilities, training staff, and many more. Our spacecrafts used for our flights are reusable and have undergone multiple successful test flights before being cleared for practical use. Additionally, we have multiple rigorous safety protocols and procedures to ensure the safety of their passengers. Moreover, we have been working closely with regulatory authorities such as the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) and the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) to ensure that our operations meet the highest safety standards. We pride ourselves in the attention to detail when it comes to safety. We want to make sure that your space flight experience is a life changing experience. Your safety is of the upmost priority.
