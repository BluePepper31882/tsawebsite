Environmental Promise Here at Space-1, we are committed to futhering our mission while protecting our environment. We are proud of our rockets being internally powered by first of their kind, Hydrogen Fuel Cells. Instead of Fossil Fuels, clean and renewable sources are converted into electricity, which is then used to power internal systems, and on board electricity grid.

We are also proud say that all water used on board our rockets are 100% recycled. From drinking grade water, to industrial grade water for fuel cell conversion, every last droplet of water is reused.

At Space-1, we hope to remain innovators in the field of environmental friendliness, and sustainable space tourism. While we look towards the stars, we must work to protect the home we have now.
